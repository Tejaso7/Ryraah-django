How to take care of silver Jewellery?

Do you love to wear sterling silver elegant Jewellers? and don't know how to take care of them and clean it. If yes, you are in the right place. Just follow these quick tips and let your stunning silver jewellery shine every day.


  •  Keep it always Clean- Please get in the habit of cleaning your jewellery when you take it off at the end of a long day. Always use soft cotton or woollen cloth, and gently rub the silver chain with it regularly to keep the silver chain shiny.

  •  Store them properly- Store silver jewellery in an airtight container or box in a dark room. Do not store them in damp places like the kitchen and bathroom. The harder you work to protect your chain from dust and ambient temperature, the longer it will look like new.

  • Avoid Moisture- Keep skin dry when wearing jewellery. If you keep silver jewellery near damp areas of your body, try dabbing a little powder on it to keep it dry. Remove silver jewellery when swimming, clean (detergent is very bad), wash hands, or touch seawater.

  • Talcum A Saviour- Polishing your silver jewellery with talcum powder and wrapping them in cloth if you don't have a carrying case or Ziplock bag will also help keep your silver from tarnishing.

  • White vinegar and baking soda: This cleaning method is ideal for removing heavy stains. Soak sterling silver in 1/2 cup of white vinegar and two tablespoons of baking powder. Prepare the fizz and foam. After that, Rinse the jewellery and tap it to dry

  • Rhodium Silver- It is Better to purchase only rhodium plated silver. It will enhance the lustre and durability of silver also gives a smooth, shiny finish to the jewellery. However, the coating can wear out sometimes, and you'll have to get a new coat every few years, but it's worth it.

  • Cornflour A miracle- If you're short on time, then do just simple things: make a corn-starch paste and dab it on with a damp cloth. Please wait for the mixture to dry, then gently rub it with a clean cloth. 

 

Proper care and maintenance are critical to keeping jewellery for a more extended period. We suggest you allow professionals to handle this or follow these above-mentioned uncomplicated tricks. These steps will let your silver earring, bracelet, Anklets, and rings, shine forever.
